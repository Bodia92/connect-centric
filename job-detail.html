Full Stack Software Engineer (.NET Core / C# )

We are looking for a Full Stack Software Engineer to join our growing company. This role details producing and maintaining software solutions that are scalable, secure, and easy to maintain while ensuring the standards and best practices are adhered to. This opportunity is ideally suited to someone passionate about technology who has a broad set of skills and readiness to dig deep into all aspects of our platform. You will have the ability to take on advanced tasks while also being a productive and collaborative team member.

Responsibilities:

  • Gain an understanding of business requirements, current system operations/functionality, and proposed new requirements.
  • Develop new and redesign existing end-to-end features of our web applications.
  • Build and enhance backend services, APIs, and web-based user interfaces.
  • Keep the code clean and review your team members’ code if needed.
  • Ensure the reliability and scalability of developed features.
  • Develop concise, articulate, and detailed technical documentation.
  • Design, build and maintain automated unit and integration tests.
  • Work with the sizeable existing codebase to identify opportunities for refactoring and removing technical debt.
  • Leverage third-party APIs to build integrations with other systems.
  • Participate in daily stand-ups and sprint planning sessions.
  • Participate in solution design activities.
