The Best Solutions
for Your Business

Connect Centric provides support for Women-Led non-profit organisations through human cantered, Powerfully simple technology and operations solutions. Whether your organisation is starting from scratch or is seeking to improve existing processes, we have the industry experience and the personal dedication to help you define your vision and meet your goals.

What We Do

Our Unique Process of Development

Consulting

Whether you need help turning your vision into an actionable plan or you need to analyze and improve specific elements of your day to day operations to yield better results for your organization, Connect Centric is ready to provide the support that works for you.

Strategy

We assist your organization in defining and executing a plan that will help you breathe easier today and be prepared for long term success. Based on your goals, requirements, and strategic vision, we’ll support you in developing a tactful approach to accomplishing your mission.

Technology

Connect Centric provides support for the full life cycle of the technology that powers your organization, from defining requirements and selecting the right solutions to implementing and supporting these solutions post-deployment.

Operations

Connect Centric helps you and your organization perform at the highest level by providing full operational support, metrics and reporting, and ongoing improvements in the areas most important to your company.

Three Important Soft Skills to Support Your Organisation

Some firms call this their secret sauce, we describe it as just the way we work.

  • Empathetic

  • Transparent

We’re Here For You
